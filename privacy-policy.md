# Privacy Policy — Gravix247

**Last updated: 14 June 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how Gravix247 ("**Gravix247**", "**we**", "**us**", or "**our**") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the Gravix247 mobile application (the "**App**"), available on Google Play.

This Privacy Policy describes our data practices. Where your consent is required under applicable law, we seek it through the App at the relevant point — for example, when you sign in, when you enable Google Drive backup for the Answer Library, or when you opt into push notification categories. If you do not agree with these practices, please do not use the App.

Gravix247 is intended for users who are **18 years of age or older**. By using the App, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old. ## 3. Information we collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate the App, personalise your learning experience, and provide the features you use.

### 3.1 Account and profile information
When you sign in using **Google Sign-In**, we receive and store:
- Your name and email address (from your Google account)
- Your profile photo (if available)

During profile setup, you also provide (all required to complete setup):
- Your mobile number
- Your UPSC exam attempt year
- Your optional subject

### 3.2 Device information
We generate a **device-linked identifier** — a one-way cryptographic (SHA-256) hash derived from your device's identifier and your account email. When linked to your account, we treat this identifier as personal data. It is used solely to:
- Detect and prevent abuse of new-user benefits and promotional offers
- Maintain security of the platform

### 3.3 Study and usage data
To provide the App's core features, we store data you generate while using the App, including:
- Study session records (Study Timer): duration, subject studied, mode, start/end times
- Streaks, coins, badges, and Dream Box progress
- Test/quiz attempts and scores (Prelims tests, Quick Revision)
- Bookmarks you save
- Performance analytics (e.g., subject-wise accuracy)

We may also use this data in an aggregated and, where feasible, de-identified form to understand how the App's features are used and to improve them.

### 3.4 User-generated content (Answer Library)
If you use the Answer Library feature to scan and store handwritten Mains answers:
- The scanned PDF files are stored in a dedicated folder within **your own Google Drive account** (with your explicit permission), and remain in your Google Drive account subject to your own Google Account settings and permissions.
- We store metadata about these files (e.g., file name, upload status, associated question) in our database to enable syncing and offline access.
- We access and process the contents of these files only to the extent technically necessary to upload, sync, store, display, or restore them at your direction. We do not otherwise view or share the contents of your Drive folder.

### 3.5 Purchase information
If you purchase access to premium content (e.g., unlocking specific Prelims subjects or test series) through Google Play Billing, we receive and store:
- The Google Play purchase token and order ID
- The plan/product purchased and its status (active, refunded, revoked)

We do **not** collect, process, or store your payment card or banking details. All payments are processed directly by Google Play under Google's own privacy and payment terms.

### 3.6 Push notification data
We use **Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)** to deliver push notifications (e.g., daily current affairs, test reminders, study tips). This requires storing a device-specific FCM token. You can control which notification categories you receive, or disable notifications entirely, from within the App's Settings. Notification delivery also depends on your device's operating system settings.

### 3.7 Information we do **not** collect
Gravix247 does not use third-party advertising networks, does not show ads, and does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not access your contacts, call logs, SMS, photos (other than what you explicitly select for scanning), or location.

Note that the infrastructure providers listed in Section 6 (e.g., Google, Supabase) may process limited technical data — such as IP address, device/OS information, or request logs — as part of operating their services. This is standard for any app built on cloud infrastructure and is governed by those providers' own privacy policies.

## 5. Legal basis for processing

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 ("**DPDP Act**"), we process your personal data on the basis of your **consent**. Consent is obtained separately for different purposes — for example, when you sign in with Google (account creation), when you enable Google Drive backup for the Answer Library (Drive access), and when you opt into push notification categories (notifications).

You may withdraw consent at any time by: disabling the relevant feature in the App's Settings, revoking permissions through your Google Account, or emailing us at dskj4178@gmail.com. Withdrawing consent for a feature may limit or disable that feature.

## 8. Data storage, security, and retention

We apply reasonable technical and organisational security measures appropriate to the sensitivity of the data, including:
- **Row Level Security (RLS)** on our database, ensuring you can only access your own data
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Screen-capture protection (`FLAG_SECURE`) on screens displaying study content

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

**Retention:**
- We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active.
- Purchase and entitlement records are retained for as long as needed to honour your purchases and for accounting or legal purposes, even after account deletion.
- If you request deletion of your account (Section 9), we will delete or anonymise your personal data within **30 days** of verifying your request, except for data we are required to retain by law (e.g., financial records) or that is needed to resolve an active dispute.

## 9. Your rights and grievance redressal

As a "Data Principal" under the DPDP Act, you have the right to:
- **Access** a summary of the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct or update** inaccurate or outdated personal data
- **Erasure** of your personal data, subject to our legal or compliance retention obligations described in Section 8
- **Withdraw consent** at any time (note: withdrawing consent may limit or disable certain features)
- **Nominate** another individual to exercise these rights on your behalf in the event of your death or incapacity
- **Register a grievance** regarding the processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at **dskj4178@gmail.com** with the subject line "Privacy Request". We will:
- Acknowledge your request within **7 days** of receipt
- Resolve your request or grievance within **90 days** of receipt, as required under the DPDP Rules, 2025

## 10. Data breach notification

We will also notify the Data Protection Board of India without delay, and provide a detailed report within **72 hours** of becoming aware of the breach, as required under the DPDP Rules, 2025.
